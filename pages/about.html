About MedicalToolKit

Empowering millions with free, accurate medical calculators and health tools.

Our Mission

MedicalToolKit exists to make healthcare information accessible to everyone. We believe that understanding your health metrics should be simple, free, and available to anyone with internet access. Our mission is to provide accurate, easy-to-use calculators and health tools that help people make informed decisions about their wellness.

Our Promise

  • Medical-Grade Accuracy: All our calculators use clinically validated formulas from reputable medical sources and organizations like the ADA, CDC, and WHO.
  • 100% Free: Every calculator is completely free to use with no registration, ads blocking functionality, or hidden charges.
  • Privacy First: We don't collect, sell, or share your personal health data. All calculations happen in your browser.
  • Mobile Optimized: Works seamlessly on phones, tablets, and desktop devices with a beautiful, responsive interface.
  • Regular Updates: We continuously improve our tools and add new calculators based on user feedback and latest medical research.

Our Tools

We currently offer 15 comprehensive health calculators covering multiple health areas:

  • Fitness: BMI, Calorie, Body Fat, Ideal Weight, TDEE, Heart Rate calculators
  • Pregnancy: Due Date, Pregnancy Week, Ovulation calculators
  • Diabetes: Blood Sugar, A1C Converter
  • Heart & Blood: Blood Pressure, GFR calculators
  • Wellness: Sleep Calculator, Anxiety Test (GAD-7)
